Privacy Policy for AirFlow App

1. Information Collection:

- Ventilation Data: AirFlow solely collects ventilation metrics and related data that you voluntarily submit through the app. This information includes measurements and readings related to indoor air quality, such as airflow rates, temperature, humidity levels, and other relevant ventilation parameters.

2. Data Usage:

- Data Purpose: The ventilation data you provide is used solely for the purpose of generating reports, analyses, and insights regarding indoor air quality. This information helps you understand and improve the ventilation in your surroundings.

3. Data Storage and Retention:

- Data Storage: All ventilation data submitted by users is securely stored in our encrypted servers. We take appropriate measures to safeguard this data and protect it against unauthorized access or disclosure.

- Data Retention: We retain the ventilation data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes mentioned above. If you choose to delete the app, your data will be promptly and permanently removed from our systems.

4. Data Sharing:

- Anonymized and Aggregated Data: We may use anonymized and aggregated ventilation data for statistical analysis, research, and improving the overall quality of our services. This data does not contain any personally identifiable information.

5. Third-Party Services:

- AirFlow does not share or transfer any user personal information or ventilation data with third-party services or entities. We maintain strict control over the data collected through the app to ensure your privacy.

6. Security Measures:

- We employ industry-standard security measures to protect the ventilation data collected through AirFlow. This includes encryption, access controls, and regular security assessments to minimize the risk of unauthorized access or misuse.

7. Children's Privacy:

- AirFlow is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. If we become aware of any such data, we will promptly delete it from our records.

8. Changes to the Privacy Policy:

- We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect any changes in our data handling practices. We encourage you to review the policy regularly. Continued use of the AirFlow app after modifications signifies your acceptance of the updated policy.

If you have any questions or concerns about our Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please contact us at contact@airflowapp.com. Your privacy is important to us, and we will address your inquiries promptly and in accordance with applicable privacy laws.
